Welcome to the UMD Gymnastics Club

Now a proud CO-ED team at the University of Maryland!

We’re open to all skill levels, from beginners to experienced gymnasts. Whether you’re just getting started or you’ve competed for years, you’ll find a supportive and competitive community here.

We offer a fun and flexible training environment with optional competitions, travel opportunities, and team-building events throughout the year.

Our team competes under USA Gymnastics Level 9 rules, but members can choose their own level based on comfort and experience. Meets are optional but encouraged!

We practice in an open-gym style format with coach-led spotting, corrections, and conditioning. Team members are welcome to train at their own pace.

Members can purchase club-branded Under Armour gear at a team discount each semester.

Stay connected and follow us on social media for team updates, photos, and announcements!

About Us

The Maryland Club Gymnastics Team is established for the expressed purpose of extending the opportunity of competitive gymnastics to all who are interested and to provide a positive gymnastics experience for all participants.

We welcome members of all levels and abilities—from beginners to Level 10 gymnasts—and compete in 2–3 meets per semester around the mid‑Atlantic, as well as attending NAIGC Nationals each April.

General Information

As of September 2017, we are a co‑ed club! Anyone can join and use both Women’s and Men’s Artistic Gymnastics equipment.

Practices are run in an open‑gym style with the first half hour as a group stretch and the last half hour optional team conditioning. Members are free to come and go, choose the events they work on, and practice skills within their range.

We have a coach who provides spotting, suggests drills, gives corrections, and leads conditioning.

No prior experience is required—we welcome members of all skill levels.

Club Fees

Season fees are $100/year or $50/semester. Fees should be paid no later than your third practice.

Competitions

Competitions are optional but strongly encouraged—they're lots of fun! The team pays for hotel and meet expenses, so fundraising is key. We attend 4–6 competitions per year, including venues like Virginia Tech, JMU, Salisbury University, our annual home meet, and Nationals in April.

We compete under USA Gymnastics Level 9 rules, although individuals may choose their own level.

Club Under Armour Apparel

Each semester, members can order club‑branded Under Armour gear (sweatshirts, leggings, tees, etc.) at up to a 50% discount.

Community Service

We give back through community service each semester—making hospital cards, running canned‑food drives, and combining service with fun team events like taco dinners or ice cream parties.

Fundraising Oppurtunities

Fundraising is one of the most important aspects of our student-run sports club. In order to compete for our team at Nationals, you are expected to participate in fundraising, which goes towards your payment for the trip.

Fundraising Requirements

  • All gymnasts are encouraged to participate in fundraising.
  • Members who are interested in, or definitely attending Nationals must participate in fundraising.
  • As a student-run club, we receive limited funding from the University—Nationals fees are the responsibility of each attending member.
  • Any money a member fundraises is applied directly to their personal Nationals cost.
  • Members who meet the fundraising goal for Nationals can typically attend the trip at no personal cost.
  • Most of our members attend Nationals for free each year by meeting their fundraising goals.
